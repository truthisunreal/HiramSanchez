§ 01 About

The work behind the work.

I work at the intersection of point-of-sale systems, SQL Server engineering, and the day-to-day technical glue that keeps an enterprise retail operation running. For the last eighteen years that's been at Duty Free Americas, where I'm the gatekeeper for POS change control and business-rule governance across 400+ registers and 200+ SQL servers running Oracle Xstore.

I'm probably most proud of POS Plus Platform — a suite of 30+ custom in-store applications I built and maintain, running 24/7 across all locations to extend Xstore functionality where the platform stops. Beyond that, my day looks like SQL schema work, ASP.NET application development, ERP integrations with Oracle Retek Resa and SIM, BI/SSRS reporting, and the occasional payment-hardware integration.

Lately I've leaned hard into AI-assisted development — Claude, GitHub Copilot, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Ollama for local models. The honest version of that story isn't "AI replaces engineers"; it's that I can now ship internal tools, automate documentation, and shorten time-to-resolution on complex incidents at a rate that wasn't possible two years ago.

I'm a calm problem-solver who likes owning things end-to-end — from debugging a register at the script level to architecting a new integration. Bilingual (English / Spanish). Based in Hialeah.

§ 03 Platform Deep Dive

POS Plus Platform.

POS Plus Platform is the suite of 30+ custom in-store applications I architected and have maintained for years, running 24/7 across every Duty Free Americas retail location. It exists because off-the-shelf POS — even something as substantial as Oracle Xstore — can't anticipate every operational reality.

When the platform falls short of what stores actually need, I build the piece that closes the gap. Then I keep it running.

The result is a layered ecosystem that sits next to Xstore — extending its functionality, monitoring its health, automating its housekeeping, and giving store associates and IT the tools off-the-shelf software didn't ship with.
